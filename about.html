About Me

Embedded systems engineer focused on hands-on hardware, robotics, and tools that make research and everyday life easier.

Who I Am

Yesenia Puga, an embedded systems engineer dedicated to building devices that operate in the real world—robots, smart sensors, and research-support tools. Computer Engineering studies at the University of California, Santa Cruz emphasized hardware design, C programming, and low-level systems.

Project work spans microcontrollers, sensors, and software systems, including an autonomous hospital sanitation robot, an ESP32-based soil moisture monitor with an accompanying iOS app, and a Python annotation tool for neuroscience images known as Fine-Tune.

Design Focus & Motivation

Motivation comes from creating systems that help people directly—supporting hospital staff through robotics, improving research workflows with better analysis tools, and empowering new learners who interact with microcontrollers for the first time. Problem-solving emphasizes breaking complex ideas into manageable steps, iterative testing, and producing solutions that are reliable, documented, and maintainable.

Skills & Strengths

  • Embedded Systems (ESP32, Arduino)
  • C / C++ / Python / MicroPython
  • I2C, SPI, UART, BLE communication
  • Robotics & state machine design
  • Image processing & ML tools
  • SwiftUI iOS integration
  • Leading workshops & mentoring students
