Professional Opportunities

Open to collaborations, internships, research roles, and projects related to embedded systems, robotics, and medical technology.

For Employers & Collaborators

Strong interest in roles involving embedded systems, microcontroller programming, robotics, and tools for healthcare or neuroscience. Enjoy working on cross-functional teams where hardware, software, and design come together to solve real-world problems.

Services & Collaboration

  • Embedded firmware development for ESP32 and related microcontrollers.
  • Sensor integration and communication protocols (I2C, SPI, UART, BLE).
  • Rapid prototyping for robotics and interactive hardware systems.
  • Python tools for data processing, image analysis, or laboratory workflows.
  • Workshops or small group sessions on microcontroller fundamentals.

Sponsorship & Support

Embedded systems workshops are offered for students to gain hands-on experience. Support for hardware, development boards, or lab materials helps expand access to engineering education. Those interested in supporting these efforts can reach out through the contact page.
